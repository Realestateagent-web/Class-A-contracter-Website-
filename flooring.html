01 — What's Included

No surprises. Everything spelled out.

Flooring install is more than laying down boards — subfloor prep, transitions, and trim make the difference. Here's our typical scope.

Included

  • Removal and disposal of existing flooring
  • Subfloor inspection, leveling, and spot-repair
  • Moisture barrier and underlayment (for LVP and engineered wood)
  • Professional install with proper expansion gaps
  • Reducer strips, thresholds, and trim at transitions
  • Quarter round or shoe molding install at baseboards
  • Full cleanup and debris haul-away
  • 2-year workmanship warranty

Priced Separately

  • Major subfloor replacement (quoted after demo if needed)
  • Stair tread refinishing or replacement
  • Furniture moving (we can move, or you can for 10% discount)
  • Baseboard replacement (common companion; separately scoped)
  • Custom inlays, borders, or medallions
  • Concrete floor grinding or major leveling beyond 1/4 inch

05 — FAQ

Questions we get every week.

If yours isn't here, scroll back up and ask our AI assistant — or just call and we'll talk it through.

Ask our AI
LVP or hardwood — what's your take?

Honest answer: for 80% of Northern Virginia homes, LVP is the smarter choice. It's waterproof, kid/pet/dog proof, looks very convincing, and costs half as much installed. Save real hardwood for formal spaces where you'll enjoy the authenticity.

Can you install over my existing floor?

Sometimes. LVP can go over many existing hard surfaces (tile, old LVP, some hardwood) if they're level and in good shape. Never over carpet. We'll evaluate during the measure — floating over existing saves demo cost but sacrifices 1/4 inch of height.

How do I transition between rooms with different flooring?

Reducers (for height difference), T-moldings (same height, different materials), thresholds (doorways). We pick the right piece per transition. Most transitions use the darker material's reducer so it blends into the rug or floor below.

Can you match my existing hardwood?

For small patches, we can usually match species and stain well enough that only we can tell. For larger work, we recommend sanding and refinishing the whole floor for a seamless look — we coordinate that with a refinishing specialist.

What about pet stains or water damage under the old floor?

We'll find it during demo. Small stains we spot-seal and kill odors. Larger damage (rot, mold) we scope as additional repair before laying new flooring over it. We never lay new over known damage.

Do we need to move furniture?

We can move standard furniture as part of the job. For pianos, pool tables, armoires, or antiques, we recommend you handle those separately. Clear smaller items (books, breakables) yourself before we arrive.

Ready for new floors?

Measure roughly and send us room dimensions. We'll ballpark same-day and schedule an on-site measure for a firm quote.
