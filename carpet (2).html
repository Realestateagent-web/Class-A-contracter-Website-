01 — What's Included

No surprises. Everything spelled out.

Carpet install is fast, but only if the prep is done right. Here's what's in a typical NWP carpet job.

Included

  • Removal and haul-away of existing carpet & pad
  • Subfloor inspection, nail-pop fix, tack strip replacement
  • New premium pad (6lb+ density typical)
  • Power-stretched carpet install (never kick-only)
  • Seams placed in low-traffic, low-visibility areas
  • Transitions at doorways, stairs, and flooring changes
  • Furniture moved and replaced (standard residential only)
  • 2-year workmanship warranty on install

Priced Separately

  • Subfloor replacement (if damaged; priced per sheet)
  • Stair carpet with custom waterfall install (more labor)
  • Pet odor treatment / subfloor sealing (add-on)
  • Premium pads (memory foam, cork underlayment)
  • Rugs bound from carpet remnants
  • Custom stair runner install (often uses different material)

05 — FAQ

Questions we get every week.

If yours isn't here, scroll back up and ask our AI assistant — or just call and we'll talk it through.

Ask our AI
Carpet or LVP for bedrooms?

Personal call. Carpet is warmer underfoot and quieter — most NoVA clients still prefer it for bedrooms. LVP is easier to clean and allergy-friendly. Split-the-difference: LVP main floor, carpet upstairs bedrooms.

What's the most durable fiber for pet households?

Solution-dyed nylon — the color is added to the raw fiber before spinning, so bleach and urine can't strip it. Triexta (SmartStrand) is another strong pet-friendly choice. Avoid polyester for heavy pet use.

Can you install over my existing hard floor?

Yes for most substrates — OSB, plywood, or concrete (with appropriate pad). We don't install over old carpet (moisture/mold risk) or over severely damaged subfloor.

What pad should I get?

Don't skimp on pad. A $5 upgrade per square yard extends your carpet's life by years. We recommend 6–8 lb density rebond pad as the standard, or memory foam for premium bedroom use.

How do you handle stairs?

Two techniques: waterfall (carpet continues straight down each step — easier, faster) or upholstered (wraps tightly around each nosing — more premium look, more labor). We'll walk you through both.

Can you clean or repair our existing carpet instead?

We don't do cleaning — we refer to carpet cleaners we trust. For repair (seam pull-aparts, patch damaged areas), yes, we can do small fixes. For large damage or heavy wear, replacement is usually better value.

Ready for new carpet?

Measure your rooms roughly and send us dimensions. We'll ballpark same-day and schedule an in-home measure for a firm quote.
