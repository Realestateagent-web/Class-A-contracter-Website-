02 — Process

A calm, predictable path from first call to final walk-through.

Remodels get messy when the plan is vague. Ours isn't. Here's exactly how your project moves from a phone call to a finished space.

01

Free virtual consult

Send a few phone photos or hop on a 15-minute video call. You get a ballpark number and an honest read on scope — before anyone drives out.

02

On-site measure & written scope

Our project lead measures, photographs, and writes a line-item scope with materials and lead times. You approve before we order a thing.

03

Build phase, managed daily

One foreman, one schedule, one group text. Dust barriers up, floors covered, site swept every afternoon. No mystery subs, no ghosted weeks.

04

Walk-through & warranty

We walk the space with you, fix the punch list on the spot, and back labor & materials with our two-year workmanship warranty.
